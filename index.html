Top three activities to do at Mexico City

Chapultepec Park

Chapultepec is one of the oldest and largest urban park in Latin America, and one of the oldest urban parks in the world.

Zócalo

If there is something that amazes locals and travelers, it's the Historical Center of Mexico City. With the Palacio Nacional or the Metropolitan Cathedral and in the background, the vestiges of the Aztec Empire.

Día de muertos

On November 1st and, mexican people build small altars in their homes with fruit, food, flowers, and small articles that belonged to their loved ones in the belief that they will come from the afterlife to visit them, in the Day of the Dead, el 'Dia de los Muertos,'.
